Tutorial for Dueling Bots

The basics

Each player starts with 500 HP (Health), and both players attack using cards until their health goes to zero.

You can view all 15 cards here.

Card Types

There are three types of cards:

  • Attack cards - deal damage to your opponent
  • Healing cards - heal yourself
  • Defense cards - protect yourself against an upcoming attack.

Card Hands

Each turn, you are given 5 random cards. There may be duplicates in any hand. The other four will be discarded when you play one card.

This being said, there is no guarantee you will get one of any specific card type.

Last, but not least, Card IDs

Cards are numbered 1-15. You can find this on the bottom right of the card. Simple, no?









Some advanced methods:

When you want to use more advanced methods (like playing specific cards):

playCardFromID(cardID)

Which plays a specific card. Before calling this, you should call:

hasCardOfID(cardID)

To make sure you can actually play the card, otherwise your bot may crash.
